From the depths of the earth, to the height of trust

Quarried with care, exported with precision.

Persia Stone produces and supplies premium stone blocks and slabs that reflect natural beauty and superior craftsmanship. Each block is carefully selected and processed to ensure exceptional quality, durability and elegance.

Our own export company in Turkey handles every formality, so factories across Europe — from Carrara to the rest of Italy — receive their material on time, every time.

About us

We are Persia Stone.

Persia Stone is inspired by the Persian history in the stone industry. For thousands of years, Persians have known how to extract stone and decorate their constructions. Rich Iranian quarries, together with genius workers, led to making this history.
